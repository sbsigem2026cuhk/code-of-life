Code of Life
The Central Dogma of Molecular Biology

All life follows a flow of genetic information. It begins with nucleotides, the building blocks that form DNA. DNA wraps around histones to create nucleosomes, which pack into chromatin and fold into chromosomes. Segments of DNA are transcribed into mRNA, which is translated into proteins. Proteins organise into functional cells, and cells come together to form a complete organism.
